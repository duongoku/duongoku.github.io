Glass bottle safety

  • Use only pressure-rated round glass bottles.
  • Leave 1–2 inches, or about 25–50 mm, of headspace.
  • Avoid very sugary juice or fruit purée in sealed bottles.
  • Check carbonation at least every 24 hours and refrigerate as soon as carbonation is adequate.
  • Chill at least 10 hours, or overnight, before opening.

Naturally carbonated drinks build pressure from yeast, sugar, warmth, and time in a sealed bottle. When in doubt, use more headspace, ferment cooler, and refrigerate earlier.
